Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how JobHub uses cookies and similar technologies on our platform.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience and allow certain features to work properly.

How Cookies Work

  • • Stored on your device (computer, phone, tablet)
  • • Sent back to our servers on subsequent visits
  • • Help remember your preferences and settings
  • • Enable personalized experiences
  • • Provide analytics and usage data

Similar Technologies

  • • Web beacons (invisible pixels)
  • • Local storage and session storage
  • • Browser fingerprinting
  • • Mobile device identifiers
  • • Server logs and analytics

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Purpose

  • • User authentication and security
  • • Session management
  • • CSRF protection
  • • Basic platform functionality

Duration

Session cookies (deleted when browser closes)

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our platform to improve performance and user experience.

Purpose

  • • Track page views and user journeys
  • • Measure platform performance
  • • Identify popular features
  • • Detect technical issues

Duration

Up to 2 years

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and settings to enhance your experience on our platform.

Purpose

  • • Remember language preferences
  • • Save search filters and settings
  • • Maintain user interface preferences
  • • Enable platform features

Duration

Up to 1 year

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Purpose

  • • Display relevant job recommendations
  • • Track marketing campaign performance
  • • Personalize content and ads
  • • Measure conversion rates

Duration

Up to 1 year

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device. These services help us provide enhanced functionality and analytics.

Analytics Providers

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Hotjar: Provides heatmaps and user behavior insights
  • Mixpanel: Tracks user engagement and feature usage

Authentication Services

  • Better Auth: Manages secure user authentication
  • Social Login Providers: Enables login via Google, LinkedIn, etc.

Customer Support

  • Intercom: Provides in-platform messaging and support
  • Help Scout: Manages customer inquiries and documentation

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options to manage cookies and control your privacy preferences on our platform.

Browser Cookie Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • • Block all cookies
  • • Block third-party cookies
  • • Delete existing cookies
  • • Receive notifications about cookies

Popular browsers:

  • • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Platform Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences directly on our platform through your account settings.

How to manage preferences:

  1. 1. Log in to your JobHub account
  2. 2. Go to Settings → Privacy & Cookies
  3. 3. Choose your preferred cookie settings
  4. 4. Save your preferences

Opt-out of Marketing Cookies

You can opt out of marketing cookies from third-party advertising networks.

Digital Advertising Alliance

Visit optout.aboutads.info

Network Advertising Initiative

Visit optout.networkadvertising.org

5. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our platform and limit available features.

Essential Cookies Disabled

If you disable essential cookies, you may not be able to log in, access your account, or use core platform features.

Analytics Cookies Disabled

Platform performance may be affected, and we may not be able to identify and fix technical issues as effectively.

Functional Cookies Disabled

Your preferences may not be saved, and you may need to reconfigure settings on each visit.

Marketing Cookies Disabled

You may see less relevant job recommendations and advertisements, but core functionality remains unaffected.
