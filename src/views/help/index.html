Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on "Sign Up" in the top navigation. Choose your role (Candidate or Employer), fill in your details including name, email, and password, then verify your email. For employers, you'll need to create a company profile after registration.

Use the search bar on the homepage or jobs page. You can search by keywords, location, company, or job type. Use filters to narrow down results by salary range, experience level, and job category.

Click on a job posting, review the details, and click "Apply Now". Upload your resume and fill in any required information. You'll receive a confirmation email, and employers will be notified of your application.

After creating an employer account and company profile, go to your dashboard and click "Post a Job". Fill in job details including title, description, requirements, salary, and location. Jobs are reviewed before being published.

Click "Forgot Password" on the sign-in page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email. The link will expire in 24 hours for security.

We accept PDF, DOC, DOCX, and TXT formats for resume uploads. Maximum file size is 5MB. Ensure your resume is up-to-date and formatted properly for best results.

Job postings are typically reviewed within 24 hours during business days. You'll receive an email notification once your job is approved and published, or if it requires revisions.

Yes, you can edit active job postings from your employer dashboard. Some changes may require re-approval. Once a job is closed or filled, it cannot be edited.
